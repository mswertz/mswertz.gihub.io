We created MOLGENIS to accelerate scientific discovery. We transforming the data and analysis solutions in easy to customize and use tools to find, capture, exchange, manage and analyse large amounts of data. MOLGENIS helps you to collaborate, structure and analyse your data, make your data FAIR and as Open as you want. It is used in many scientific areas such as biobanking, rare disease research, patient registries (and even energy research!) and other scientific organisations all around the world
